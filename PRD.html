VetAdopt

A pet adoption and rehoming marketplace for Jabodetabek. Shelters and verified owners list animals that need a home. Adopters apply — not buy. Matching the right pet with the right household replaces the transaction.

01

Problem & Opportunity

Indonesia has millions of stray and surrendered animals in need of homes, yet the adoption process remains fragmented and informal. Discovery happens through Facebook groups, WhatsApp broadcast lists, and word of mouth — channels with no screening, no trust signals, and no status visibility.

The result: shelters are overwhelmed with animals they cannot place, serious adopters cannot find them efficiently, and private rehomers have no credible channel to find a vetted new household for a pet they love.

The core dysfunction

Every step of the current process — discovery, evaluation, application, communication — relies on manual effort and informal trust. There is no structured way for a shelter to assess an applicant's lifestyle fit, no way for an adopter to track where their application stands, and no trust layer that distinguishes a responsible rescue organisation from an anonymous post.

The opportunity is not to digitise adoption paperwork. It is to make the match better and the trust legible — so more animals reach households that can genuinely care for them.

Why Jabodetabek first

Jakarta metro concentrates the highest density of registered animal shelters, the strongest consumer smartphone penetration, and the most feasible meet-and-greet logistics. Starting here allows a focused supply-side relationship with real shelters before attempting national scale.

02

Vision & Goals

VetAdopt pairs animals that need homes with households that can care for them, replacing a scattered and informal process with a structured, trustworthy one — without losing the warmth of what adoption actually is.

What "success" looks like for each party

Adopter Finds an animal whose temperament and needs genuinely fit their household. Applies with confidence, knows where they stand at each stage, and reaches the meet-and-greet with the right expectations set.
Shelter admin Lists animals once and receives pre-screened applications with lifestyle data attached. Spends time on relationship-building and decisions, not on fielding unqualified enquiries.
Private rehomer Has a credible, verified channel to find a new home for a pet they care about. Is seen as trustworthy despite not being an institution.

– In-app messaging between adopter and lister
– Video or virtual meet-and-greet

– Foster programme listings
– Rabbit, birds, or other small animals
– Cities outside Jabodetabek
– Native mobile apps (web-first)
– Medical record management
– Analytics dashboard for shelter admins
– Push notification infrastructure
– Email notification system
– Multi-admin accounts per shelter

Lifestyle-match filtering

Filters represent adopter household context, not pet attributes. The system compares declared lifestyle against pet temperament tags and lister-specified requirements to generate a visual match score per listing.

Filter selections save as the adopter's "Match Profile." Returning visitors get pre-filtered results immediately. Filters are shown as a persistent side panel on desktop and a bottom sheet on mobile.

Adoption application form

Multi-step Application 4 steps
Step 1 — Informasi pelamar Nama lengkap, email, nomor telepon, wilayah Jabodetabek, status hunian (sewa/milik sendiri). All required.
Step 2 — Profil kehidupan Home type, household composition, other pets, daily hours home alone, activity level, pet experience, garden/outdoor access. These are the lifestyle screening fields and feed the match score.
Step 3 — Pernyataan pribadi Two required free-text fields: "Mengapa Anda ingin mengadopsi hewan ini?" (min 100 chars) and "Ceritakan rutinitas harian Anda bersama hewan peliharaan." (min 80 chars). One optional: "Apakah ada yang perlu kami ketahui?"
Step 4 — Ringkasan & donasi Full application review. Optional donation prompt: Rp 25.000 / Rp 50.000 / Rp 100.000 / jumlah lain. Donation goes 100% to the shelter. Prototype: UI only, no real payment. Submit button triggers status = Submitted.

WhatsApp contact model

Communication Logic WA deep link
Shelter public profile Shelter's WA number is always visible on their public profile for general enquiries (not application-specific).
Before Meet & Greet WA number is hidden from the applicant. Application status page shows "Anda akan mendapat kontak lanjut setelah seleksi awal."
At Meet & Greet stage WA deep link is revealed only to the advancing applicant. One-click opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled opening message. All other applicants remain in Under Review.
Private rehomer WA Never shown publicly. Shared only at Meet & Greet, same logic as shelters.

Donation UI UI only in prototype

Default stateNo amount pre-selected. Skipping donation does not affect application processing.
AmountsRp 25.000, Rp 50.000, Rp 100.000, custom input field.
Recipient100% to the shelter or private lister. Platform operational fee decision deferred to v1.1.

07

Application Status Lifecycle

Every application moves through a defined set of states. The adopter sees their current state on a persistent status page. The shelter advances or terminates states from their application inbox.

01
Terkirim
Application submitted, confirmation shown
02
Ditinjau
Shelter reviewing lifestyle data and statement
03
Meet & Greet
WA contact revealed, visit to be arranged
04
Disetujui
Adoption approved, handover in progress
05
Diadopsi
Listing closed, animal has a home

Terminal states

Ditolak (Rejected) Shelter rejects the application. Optional rejection reason shown to the applicant. Adopter is notified and can apply to other listings. Listing remains active.
Dibatalkan (Withdrawn) Adopter withdraws their own application from the status page at any stage before Disetujui.

Only one application per pet per adopter. An adopter can apply to multiple different pets simultaneously. Once a pet is marked Diadopsi, all other pending applications for that pet are automatically closed with a neutral notification.

Trust & Verification

A shelter and a private rehomer must read differently and credibly. Trust surfaces are the primary mechanism for this — the platform cannot verify everything, so it must show clearly what it has verified.

Shelter verification

Required at registrationOrganisation name, registered address, Nomor Induk Berusaha (NIB) or equivalent, WhatsApp number, primary contact name.
Verification processManual review by VetAdopt team (v1). Target: 48-hour turnaround. Approve or request additional documentation.
Verified badgeBlue-tone badge on shelter profile: "Organisasi Terverifikasi." Displayed on every listing card from this shelter.
Public profile signalsVerified badge, adoption success count, active listing count, member since date, average response time (manual estimate in v1).

Private rehomer verification

Required at registrationFull name, KTP number (Indonesian national ID), WhatsApp number. ID not stored — hash only.
Verification processBasic ID format validation in v1. No document upload required at prototype stage.
Verified badgeAmber-tone badge: "Individu Terverifikasi." Distinguishes from unverified accounts if unverified accounts are added in future.
Rehoming storyRequired field on the listing: why they are rehoming. Displayed prominently on the pet profile. Transparency is itself a trust signal.
WA visibilityNever public. Revealed only at meet-and-greet stage to the selected applicant.

In v1, verification is lightweight by design. The goal is to create visible accountability, not to build a KYC system. The verification badge signals that VetAdopt has a record of who the lister is — not that the platform vouches for them completely.

Design Principles

Palette Coral primary oklch(0.598 0.155 28.8) + honey yellow accent oklch(0.855 0.12 88) on warm off-white. No monochrome schemes.
Typography Fraunces Variable for display headlines and pet names. Geist Variable for all UI, body, and data text. Never Inter, never system sans as primary.
Pet profiles feel like introductions A pet listing should feel like meeting an animal, not browsing inventory. The name is the headline. The story comes before the specs. Photos show personality, not just appearance.
Trust surfaces work hard Verification badges and signals must be prominent and consistent. If something is verified, say what was verified. If something is unverified, don't display a badge — absence communicates clearly.
Applications feel purposeful The multi-step form should communicate that this matters. Copy that acknowledges the animal's wellbeing. Progress that respects the adopter's time. No generic form aesthetics.
Adoption is an emotional moment The status transition to "Diadopsi" and the adoption confirmation screen should be warm, not transactional. No cart confirmation patterns. This is the end of a process that started with care.
Motion is purposeful Status transitions animate. Application steps progress smoothly. Lazy list loads stagger. Nothing bounces without reason.
Images Product closeups of the actual animals only. Sourced from Unsplash or Pinterest. No lifestyle filler, no stock "happy family with dog" imagery.
Copywriting Bahasa Indonesia throughout. No em dashes or hyphens as sentence connectors. Warm and reachable voice — this is for pet owners, not professionals. Never use "Seamless," "Solusi," or "Platform terdepan" constructions.

12

Risks & Open Questions

01
Supply-side cold start High
Without shelters, there are no listings. Without listings, no adopters come. The platform needs a minimum of 8–10 active shelters before a public launch is credible. This requires direct relationship-building with Jakarta-area rescue organisations before engineering begins. Consider a private beta period with 3–5 hand-picked shelters.
02
WA-only communication leaves no record Medium
Once WA contact is shared, all communication leaves the platform. There is no record for dispute resolution if a meet-and-greet goes wrong or a rehoming claim is made falsely. In v1 this is a calculated trade-off for simplicity. For v2, consider an optional in-platform note log that both parties can append to after the WA link is revealed.
03
Private rehomer verification is lightweight Medium
KTP number validation (format check only) does not prevent bad actors from posing as legitimate rehomers. The platform mitigates this by making WA private until meet-and-greet and by requiring a mandatory rehoming story. A scam attempt would need to invest meaningful effort to appear credible. For v1.1, consider requiring a brief video introduction.
04
Donation flow may trigger regulatory questions Medium
Collecting money on behalf of third-party organisations (shelters) in Indonesia may require a payment services licence or a specific charitable solicitation framework. The prototype UI-only approach is safe. Real donation processing needs legal review before v1.1. GoPay and QRIS integration should be scoped with compliance guidance.
05
Multiple concurrent applications per pet — UX clarity Low
When a shelter advances one applicant to Meet & Greet, the other applicants in Under Review see no change. They might not know they have effectively been deprioritised. Decide whether: (a) advancing one applicant auto-rejects others, (b) advances are private and others remain open until an explicit decision, or (c) the lister advances to Meet & Greet with one person but keeps others technically live. The answer shapes the inbox UX significantly.
06
Match score algorithm fairness Low
The lifestyle match score compares adopter self-reported data against lister-specified temperament tags. Both sides may under-specify or over-specify. A score of "High match" could create false confidence. In v1, present the score as a guide, not a guarantee. Label it as "Cocok untuk profil Anda" rather than a numerical percentage.

Decisions Log

Decisions made during the product definition interview on 27 Mei 2026. These are not implementation details — they are scoping choices that should not be revisited without a clear reason.

Geographic scope
Jabodetabek only
Enables meet-and-greet logistics and focused shelter partnerships before attempting national scale.
Animal species
Dogs and cats only
Highest adoption demand in Indonesia. Rabbit and small animal niches deferred to v1.1.
Lister types
Shelters + verified private
Broader supply than shelters-only while maintaining accountability via lightweight ID verification.
Monetisation
Optional adoption donation
Aligns incentives with outcomes (successful adoption = donation). No friction for adopters who choose not to donate.
Application depth
Lifestyle screening form
More signal than a simple intent form without the drop-off risk of a full questionnaire. Balanced friction.
Communication model
WhatsApp deep link only
Matches existing behaviour of both shelters and adopters in Indonesia. Zero messaging infrastructure to build in v1.
Product name
VetAdopt
Retains existing repo and brand equity. Signals veterinary-adjacent credibility while the Adopt suffix clarifies the core action.
Platform type
Web-first, no native apps
Reduces build scope for prototype. TanStack Start (React + Nitro) is the existing stack and handles SSR for SEO on listing pages.
